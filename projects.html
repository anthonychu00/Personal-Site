Reddit is one of the largest forums for discussion on the Internet, and as a result a variety of stances and points of views are represented on the platform. Using the Reddit API and the Sentiment package from Node, I created a small web app that lets you search a keyword and see what Reddit's stance (positive or negative) is on that particular keyword. Posts on Reddit are normally neutral or close to neutral in tone, so most posts are neutral, or close to 0, according to the sentiment score.
